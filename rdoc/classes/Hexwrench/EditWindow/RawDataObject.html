Class Hexwrench::EditWindow::RawDataObject
In: lib/hexwrench/edit_window.rb
Parent: Wx::DataObject

Clipboard notes: Wx::Clipboard is not quite stable yet. Specifically the copy/pasting of raw binary data with non-ascii bytes is buggy and inconsistent between platforms.

This is a clipboard format that will work reliably within the app but does not to/from other apps.

XXX We can‘t use Wx::DF_TEXT or other externally compatible formats until (hopefully) problems are addressed in a future wxruby version.
